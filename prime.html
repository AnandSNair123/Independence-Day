1. Jawaharlal Nehru
Born-Dead : (1889–1964)
Term of Office: 15 August 1947 to 27 May 1964 16 years, 286 days
Remarks: The first prime minister of India and the longest-serving PM of India, the first to die in office.

2. Gulzarilal Nanda
Born-Dead : (1898-1998)
Term of Office: 27 May 1964 to 9 June 1964, 13 days
Remarks: First acting PM of India

3. Lal Bahadur Shastri
Born-Dead : (1904–1966)
Term of Office: 9 June 1964 to 11 January 1966 1 year, 216 days
Remarks: He has given the slogan of 'Jai Jawan Jai Kisan' during the Indo-Pak war of 1965

4. Gulzari Lal Nanda
Born-Dead : (1898-1998)
Term of Office: 11 January 1966 to 24 January 1966 13 days
Remarks: -

5. Indira Gandhi
Born-Dead : (1917–1984)
Term of Office: 24 January 1966 to 24 March 1977 11 years, 59 days
Remarks: First female Prime Minister of India

6. Morarji Desai
Born-Dead : (1896–1995)
Term of Office: 15 August 1947 to 27 May 1964 2 year, 126 days
Remarks: Oldest to become PM (81 years old) and first to resign from office

7. Charan Singh
Born-Dead : (1902–1987)
Term of Office: 28 July 1979 to 14 January 1980 170 days
Remarks: Only PM who did not face the Parliament

8. Indira Gandhi
Born-Dead : (1917–1984)
Term of Office: 14 January 1980 to 31 October 1984 4 years, 291 days
Remarks: The first lady who served as PM for the second term

9. Rajiv Gandhi
Born-Dead : (1944–1991)
Term of Office: 31 October 1984 to 2 December 1989 5 years, 32 days
Remarks: Youngest to become PM (40 years old)

10. V. P. Singh
Born-Dead : (1931–2008)
Term of Office: 2 December 1989 to 10 November 1990 343 days
Remarks: First PM to step down after a vote of no confidence

11. Chandra Shekhar
Born-Dead : (1927–2007)
Term of Office: 10 November 1990 to 21 June 1991 223 days
Remarks: He belongs to Samajwadi Janata Party

12. P. V. Narasimha Rao
Born-Dead : (1921–2004)
Term of Office: 21 June 1991 to 16 May 1996 4 years, 330 days
Remarks: First PM from south India

13. Atal Bihari Vajpayee
Born-Dead : (1924- 2018)
Term of Office: 16 May 1996 to 1 June 1996 16 days
Remarks: PM for shortest tenure

14. H. D. Deve Gowda
Born-Dead : (born 1933)
Term of Office: 1 June 1996 to 21 April 1997 324 days
Remarks: He belongs to Janata Dal

15. Inder Kumar Gujral
Born-Dead : (1919–2012)
Term of Office: 21 April 1997 to 19 March 1998 332 days
Remarks: -

16. Atal Bihari Vajpayee
Born-Dead : (1924-2018)
Term of Office: 19 March 1998 to 22 May 2004 6 years, 64 days
Remarks: The first non-congress PM who completed a full term as PM

17. Manmohan Singh
Born-Dead : (born 1932)
Term of Office: 22 May 2004 to 26 May 2014 10 years, 4 days
Remarks: First Sikh PM

18. Narendra Modi
Born-Dead : (born 1950)
Term of Office: 26 May 2014 - Present
Remarks: 4th Prime Minister of India who served two consecutive tenures
